Amy Rogan

The Empathetic Designer + Thinker

I'm passionate. Whether designing, researching, cooking, coaching rugby, or making a latte-I give it my all.

I aim to please for friends and family, so it comes naturally to genuinely care about a user's experience.

I don't believe there is ever only one right answer.

I strive to look at everything from multiple perspectives.

I strongly feel that great design begins with much thought, planning and research.



As a designer, I love clean, attractive, breathable design. Even on more elaborate designs, I aim to communicate the information in the most clear, concise way.
